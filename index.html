Bandajje Falls

One of the most spectacular waterfalls in Karnataka that is about 200 feet high plunge nestled in Belthangady taluk of Dakshina Kannada district is a must to visit attraction in the state. Nestled in the lap of dense forests and grasslands, the waterfalls can be reached only by trekking and is worth it. Immerse in the beauty of lush green dense forests, mesmerising Ghats, breath-taking sunsets, melodies of the chirping birds and the fragrance of wild flora only at the trek route towards the Bandaje falls.

Netravati Peak

Netravati Trek is located in Chikmagalur district and is sandwiched between Samse and Belathangady. The Netravati Trek gets its name from the Netravathi River, which originates at the base of the Netravati peak.

About This Blog

“Traveling leaves you speechless, but then turns you into a storyteller.”

When one travels or treks, It is an experience that they want to share with others. They want to tell a story of the things they have seen, the people they met, and the culture they have experienced. Most people tell the story through photographs, and videos they captured. Through this, they are able to express the thrill and joy of their travel experience. It is not about bragging but it is about sharing the beauty of our surrounding.
